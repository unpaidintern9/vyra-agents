# Operations Agent Workflows

## approval-queue-review

Track high-risk actions waiting for human approval.

## workflow-health-review

Review workflow freshness, failure states, and blocked local dry checks.

## integration-health-review

Collect status from planned integrations once read-only connectors exist.

## deployment-readiness-review

Prepare deployment readiness notes without deploying.

## incident-review

Review incident notes, logs, and follow-up needs without running incident automation.
