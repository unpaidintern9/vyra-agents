# Marketing Agent Hardening

- Treat audience notes, campaign performance, customer quotes, and launch plans as sensitive.
- Do not publish campaigns, send email, alter website content, upload audiences, or change ad spend from this phase.
- Keep generated content and campaign recommendations in draft form only.
- Avoid exposing private customer feedback or support details in marketing summaries.
- Escalate launch, legal, pricing, and customer-impacting decisions to human approval.
