# Future AI

AI is intentionally not implemented in this MVP.

Future AI support should be optional, metered, logged, and permission-controlled. AI should summarize, classify, draft, and recommend before it is allowed to trigger real actions.

## Guardrails

- No hidden model calls.
- No production action without workflow logging.
- No customer-facing communication without approval.
- No production data mutation without explicit permission.
- No unlogged reasoning or generated recommendations.
