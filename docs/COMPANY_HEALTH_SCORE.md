# Company Health Score

The Company Health Score is a deterministic local composite from revenue health, sales pipeline, customer success health, marketing readiness, engineering readiness, executive goal progress, task/workflow health, and risk severity.

It includes:

- score
- label
- confidence
- explanation
- risks
- recommendations
- next actions

The score is advisory. It does not approve work, sync data, trigger outreach, change billing, deploy software, publish content, or execute external actions.
