# Brand Consistency Checks

Brand consistency checks evaluate each Marketing Content Studio draft against:

- brand voice
- approved messaging
- product positioning
- audience fit
- words to avoid
- claim safety
- pricing consistency
- call-to-action clarity

Each check returns score, confidence, risks, violations, recommendations, and next actions.

Checks are advisory. A high score does not publish content, approve claims, send email, post to social, buy ads, or sync CRM records.
