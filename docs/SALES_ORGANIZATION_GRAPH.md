# Sales Organization Graph

The Organization Graph turns local Sales records into per-organization intelligence profiles.

## Organization Profile

Each profile includes:

- Organization name.
- Relationship depth.
- Active opportunity status.
- Connected proposal IDs.
- Connected dossier IDs.
- Connected follow-up IDs.
- Migration readiness.
- Intelligence completeness score.

## Organization Timeline

Timeline events are local-only and include:

- Intake created.
- Research completed.
- Proposal drafted.
- Follow-up scheduled.
- Migration planned.
- Executive review.

## Dashboard

The Sales page shows:

- Organization profile.
- Relationship graph list/tree view.
- Timeline.
- Connected records.
- Migration readiness.
- Export buttons for Organization Intelligence Report, Organization Timeline, and Sales Intelligence Graph JSON.

## Executive Signals

The Executive Dashboard summarizes:

- Organizations tracked.
- Active opportunities.
- Average relationship depth.
- Proposal coverage.
- Migration readiness.
- Intelligence completeness score.

## Safety Boundaries

- No external browsing.
- No automated scraping.
- No email sends.
- No Stripe invoices.
- No CRM writes.
- No production business writes.
- All organization intelligence remains local/mock/read-only.
