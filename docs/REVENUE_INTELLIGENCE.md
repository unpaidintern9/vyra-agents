# Revenue Intelligence

Revenue intelligence is deterministic and local-only. It estimates MRR, ARR, expansion revenue, pipeline revenue, renewal health, churn exposure, and revenue risk from local Customer Success and planning records.

Revenue records include organization identity, customer type, product, plan, subscription state, monthly amount, annual amount, setup or migration revenue, expansion potential, lifetime value estimate, forecast confidence, risks, recommendations, and next actions.

The dashboard exposes Finance, Executive, Sales, and Customer Success views. These views are advisory and do not mutate billing, Stripe, CRM, accounting, or customer records.
