# Marketing Campaigns

Campaign planner records track local campaign strategy for Vyra Performance.

Each campaign includes campaign ID, name, objective, audience, products, channels, timeline, messaging, tasks, approvals, KPIs, status, risks, and next actions.

Supported statuses:

- draft
- planning
- ready
- active
- paused
- completed
- archived

Campaigns remain local planning records. The Marketing Agent does not publish content, post to social, send email, buy ads, sync CRM records, or approve campaigns automatically.
