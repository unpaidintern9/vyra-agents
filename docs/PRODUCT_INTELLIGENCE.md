# Product Intelligence

Product intelligence tracks local marketing positioning for:

- Athlete App
- Coach Platform
- Gym Software
- White Label Platform
- Sales/CRM tools
- Future Vyra products

Each product stores audience, features, pricing status, positioning, differentiators, FAQs, launch status, linked assets, linked goals, and linked campaigns.

Pricing is marked unconfirmed where no local source exists. The Marketing Agent does not invent public pricing or unsupported launch claims.
