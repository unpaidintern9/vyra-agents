# Onboarding Engine

The Customer Success Onboarding Engine creates local onboarding plans for coaches, gyms, sports organizations, schools, enterprise organizations, and future customer types.

Templates contain stages, milestones, required tasks, required assets, training, documentation, and completion rules.

Current template families:

- Coach Onboarding.
- Gym Onboarding.
- Enterprise Onboarding.

Onboarding plans track progress, training completion, documentation usage, required assets, linked tasks, linked goals, and next actions.

No account setup, invitations, imports, billing, messaging, or external system updates happen automatically.
