# Revenue Forecasting

Revenue forecasting uses local deterministic rules over revenue references, subscription state, expansion estimates, churn exposure, renewal readiness, and confidence.

Generated reports include Revenue Summary, MRR Report, ARR Report, Revenue Forecast, Pricing Summary, Renewal Report, Expansion Report, Revenue Risk Report, and Executive Finance Summary.

Forecasts are explainable planning outputs. They do not trigger payment collection, billing changes, invoice sending, accounting synchronization, CRM writes, or executive approval.
