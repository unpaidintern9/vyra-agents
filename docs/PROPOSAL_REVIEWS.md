# Proposal Reviews

Proposal reviews are local review-stage records.

Supported stages:

- Author Review
- Peer Review
- Pink Team
- Red Team
- Gold Team
- Executive Review

Each review stores reviewer, findings, risks, recommendations, unresolved issues, and approval status.

Review records are advisory and local. They do not approve Executive gates automatically, submit proposals, send emails, sync CRM data, or trigger external actions.
