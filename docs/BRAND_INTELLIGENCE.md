# Brand Intelligence

Brand intelligence stores confirmed local Vyra Performance brand references and explicit gaps.

Confirmed local references:

- Dashboard V brand mark in `dashboard/src/styles.css`.
- Dashboard color tokens in `dashboard/src/styles.css`.
- Dashboard typography stack in `dashboard/src/styles.css`.

Missing local references are recorded instead of invented:

- Standalone logo image files.
- Product screenshots.

The brand record includes voice, approved messaging, words to use, words to avoid, and visual style notes. Brand output remains draft-only until reviewed.

## Content Studio Checks

Phase 56 runs brand consistency checks against Content Studio drafts. Checks compare drafts to brand voice, approved messaging, product positioning, audience fit, avoided words, claim safety, pricing consistency, and call-to-action clarity.

Checks are advisory and local. They do not approve claims or publish content.
