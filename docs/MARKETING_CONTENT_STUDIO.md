# Marketing Content Studio

Phase 56 adds a local-only Marketing Content Studio for Vyra Performance.

The studio creates and manages draft-only marketing assets:

- campaign briefs
- landing page drafts
- email drafts
- newsletter drafts
- social post sets
- blog outlines
- launch announcements
- release notes
- product messaging drafts
- ad copy drafts
- video and podcast briefs
- case study outlines
- FAQ drafts

Drafts live under `codex-agent-threads/shared/marketing/content-studio/`.

Every draft links to the local brand record, product record, audience profile, campaign or content record, operator, source inputs, tasks, and goals where available.

Drafts do not publish, post, email, buy ads, sync CRM data, or create external actions.
