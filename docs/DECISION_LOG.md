# Decision Log

The Executive Decision Log records local decisions with type, rationale, consequences, related goal, task, workflow, opportunity, entity, decision maker, follow-up tasks, and audit history.

Decision types:

- approval
- rejection
- prioritization
- strategic direction
- resource allocation
- risk acceptance
- pause
- resume
- archive
- general

Decision log entries are records of human or local planning decisions. They do not approve external actions automatically.
