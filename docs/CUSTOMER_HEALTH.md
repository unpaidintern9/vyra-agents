# Customer Health

Customer health evaluations are deterministic local records.

Each evaluation includes:

- Health score.
- Adoption score.
- Engagement score.
- Risk score.
- Expansion opportunity.
- Confidence.
- Reasons.
- Risks.
- Recommendations.
- Next actions.

Health monitoring covers onboarding progress, product adoption, engagement, training completion, documentation usage, feature adoption, expansion potential, and churn risk.

Scores are advisory. They do not trigger automatic outreach, account updates, renewals, CRM sync, or support responses.
