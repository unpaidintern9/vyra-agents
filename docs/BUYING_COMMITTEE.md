# Buying Committee

Phase 51 models buying committees for local Sales opportunities.

## Roles

Supported committee roles include:

- Economic buyer
- Decision maker
- Champion
- Influencer
- Technical evaluator
- Legal or compliance reviewer
- Finance reviewer
- End user
- Blocker
- Unknown

## Completeness

Buying committee completeness is deterministic. It considers whether an organization has known contacts, decision maker coverage, champion coverage, influencer coverage, missing roles, relationship strength, and confidence.

The output is advisory and explains:

- Score
- Label
- Confidence
- Covered roles
- Missing roles
- Risks
- Recommended next action

## Proposal Readiness

Proposal relationship readiness depends on buying committee completeness, decision maker coverage, relationship health, verified research, and unresolved workflow blockers.

No proposal is submitted automatically.
