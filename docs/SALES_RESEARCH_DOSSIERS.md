# Sales Research Dossiers

Research dossiers are deterministic local planning artifacts generated from saved prospect intake records.

## Dossier Sections

Each dossier includes:

- Business overview.
- ICP fit.
- Likely pain points.
- Recommended Vyra product.
- Migration opportunity.
- Outreach angle.
- Proposal angle.
- Missing info.
- Risks.
- Next steps.

## Fit Score

The fit score is explainable and based on:

- Business type.
- Estimated members.
- Migration complexity.
- Captured pain points.
- Contact path.
- Missing information.

MMA/BJJ, CrossFit, and small gym prospects receive stronger first-target weighting. Missing contact paths, unknown migration complexity, and incomplete intake fields reduce confidence.

## Exports

Dossiers can be exported locally as:

- Markdown.
- JSON.

Exports include the safety note from the local report exporter and do not send data anywhere.

## Safety Boundaries

- No AI generation.
- No external browsing.
- No automated web or social scraping.
- No emails sent.
- No Stripe invoices created.
- No CRM writes.
- No production business writes.
- Dossiers are draft planning artifacts only.
